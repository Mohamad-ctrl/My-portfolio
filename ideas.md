<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Neo-Brutalism / High-Contrast Minimalist
  **Core Principles**:
  1.  **Raw & Honest**: Expose the structure, use bold lines and high contrast to reflect the engineering nature of the work.
  2.  **Function over Form**: Prioritize readability and direct access to information (projects, skills).
  3.  **Bold Typography**: Use large, impactful headings to make a statement.
  4.  **Grid-Based Precision**: A strict, visible grid system that mimics technical blueprints or code editors.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  -   **Monochrome Base**: Black (#1a1a1a) and White (#f5f5f5) for maximum contrast and readability.
  -   **Accent**: Electric Blue (#2563eb) or Neon Green (#00ff00) to represent code execution and active states.
  -   **Intent**: To convey precision, technical expertise, and a no-nonsense approach to software engineering.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  -   **Asymmetric Grid**: A bento-box style layout for the project showcase, but with varying cell sizes and thick borders.
  -   **Sidebar Navigation**: A persistent, sticky sidebar on the left (desktop) or bottom (mobile) that looks like a code editor file tree.

  **Signature Elements**:
  -   **Thick Borders**: 2px-4px solid borders on all cards and sections.
  -   **Monospace Fonts**: Use a coding font for metadata (dates, tags) and a geometric sans-serif for headings.
  -   **Raw Shadows**: Hard, non-blurred shadows (box-shadow: 4px 4px 0px #000) to give a tactile, sticker-like feel.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  -   **Hover States**: Elements shift position (translate) on hover rather than fading. Buttons depress physically.
  -   **Cursor Effects**: A custom cursor that changes shape when hovering over interactive elements (e.g., turns into a terminal caret).

  **Animation**:
  -   **Snap Transitions**: Instant, snappy movements. No soft fades.
  -   **Typewriter Effect**: For the hero section introduction.

  **Typography System**:
  -   **Headings**: 'Space Grotesk' or 'Chakra Petch' (Bold, Uppercase).
  -   **Body**: 'Inter' or 'Public Sans' (Clean, readable).
  -   **Code/Meta**: 'JetBrains Mono' or 'Fira Code'.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Glassmorphism / Ethereal Tech
  **Core Principles**:
  1.  **Depth & Layering**: Use translucency and blur to create a sense of depth, mimicking modern OS interfaces (macOS, iOS).
  2.  **Soft & Fluid**: Rounded corners, smooth gradients, and organic shapes to contrast with the rigid nature of code.
  3.  **Light & Airy**: A focus on light mode with subtle colorful glows to feel welcoming and innovative.
  4.  **Content-First**: The UI recedes to let the project images and content shine.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  -   **Background**: Soft, pastel gradients (Aurora borealis style - teal, violet, soft blue) on a white/light gray base.
  -   **Glass**: White with low opacity and backdrop-blur for cards and nav.
  -   **Text**: Dark Slate (#334155) for softer contrast than pure black.
  -   **Intent**: To present the developer as modern, forward-thinking, and user-experience focused.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  -   **Floating Cards**: Content exists on "floating" glass cards above a subtle, animated background.
  -   **Central Focus**: A centered, single-column flow for the main narrative, with expanding cards for details.

  **Signature Elements**:
  -   **Frosted Glass**: `backdrop-filter: blur(12px)` on all containers.
  -   **Soft Glows**: Behind key elements (hero image, CTA buttons) to highlight them.
  -   **Rounded Corners**: Large border-radius (xl or 2xl) for a friendly, approachable feel.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  -   **Parallax**: Subtle movement of background elements against the foreground cards on scroll.
  -   **Glow on Hover**: Borders light up or glow intensifies when interacting with cards.

  **Animation**:
  -   **Float**: Gentle vertical floating animation for hero elements.
  -   **Fade & Slide**: Smooth entry animations for content as it scrolls into view.

  **Typography System**:
  -   **Headings**: 'Outfit' or 'Plus Jakarta Sans' (Geometric, friendly).
  -   **Body**: 'DM Sans' or 'Quicksand'.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Swiss Style / International Typographic Style (Digital Adaptation)
  **Core Principles**:
  1.  **Grid Systems**: A mathematical, rigid grid is the foundation of the layout.
  2.  **Asymmetry**: Balancing elements through asymmetry rather than centering.
  3.  **Typography as Image**: Type is the primary design element, used large and bold.
  4.  **Negative Space**: Extensive use of white space to create breathing room and focus.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  -   **Palette**: Stark Black and White with a single, bold accent color (e.g., International Orange #FF4F00 or Swiss Red #D80000).
  -   **Intent**: To convey clarity, objectivity, and a structured, architectural approach to development.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  -   **Split Screen**: Often uses a split layout (text on one side, visual/image on the other) or a 1/3 - 2/3 ratio.
  -   **Horizontal Rhythm**: Strong horizontal lines separating sections.

  **Signature Elements**:
  -   **Huge Typography**: Massive font sizes for section headers (e.g., "PROJECTS", "ABOUT").
  -   **Rule Lines**: Thin, precise horizontal and vertical lines to define the grid.
  -   **Minimalist Icons**: Simple, stroke-based icons if used at all.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  -   **Micro-interactions**: Subtle color shifts on hover (e.g., black text turns orange).
  -   **Smooth Scroll**: A very smooth, perhaps slightly damped scrolling experience.

  **Animation**:
  -   **Reveal**: Text reveals from behind masks (clipping paths).
  -   **Line Drawing**: Grid lines draw themselves upon scroll.

  **Typography System**:
  -   **Headings**: 'Helvetica Now' (or 'Inter' Tight/Display) - Classic, neutral, bold.
  -   **Body**: 'Roboto' or 'Arial' - Neutral, legible.
</idea>
</text>
